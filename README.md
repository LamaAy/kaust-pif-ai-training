#  KAUST–PIF AI Training Program

This repository contains the official materials for the KAUST–PIF Artificial Intelligence Training Program, a structured and hands-on initiative designed to build AI literacy, technical competence, and applied skills for diverse professional backgrounds.

The program is organized into two progressive levels, starting with AI awareness and adoption and advancing toward full technical implementation of modern AI systems.

## Program Overview

Level 1 – AI Literacy & Adoption  
Focus: AI literacy, data lifecycle, and AI adoption  
Outcome: Participants understand core AI concepts, the full data lifecycle, responsible AI use, and how AI is adopted in organizations through non-technical and interactive activities.

Level 2 – Technical AI & Applications  
Focus: Python, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Natural Language Processing  
Outcome: Participants build, train, and evaluate AI models through rich hands-on labs and applied projects.
